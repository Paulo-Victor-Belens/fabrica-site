# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 20, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Fabrica ("we", "us", operating the website
https://www.fabricatemplates.com) handles information in connection with our
internal application, Fabrica Shop Manager.

## Who we are

Fabrica is a small business that sells digital templates and printables. The
Fabrica Shop Manager application is an internal tool used **only by us**, to
manage **our own** Pinterest business account and Etsy shop. It is not offered
to, and does not serve, any third-party users.

## What data we access

When we connect our own Pinterest account through Pinterest's official API, the
application may access:

- Our own Pinterest boards and Pins
- Our own account analytics (impressions, clicks, saves)
- Basic account profile information

We do **not** access, collect, or process data belonging to any other Pinterest
users.

## How we use the data

The data is used solely to schedule and publish Pins to our own boards and to
measure the performance of our own content. We do not sell, rent, or share this
data with any third party.

## How we store and protect it

Access tokens obtained through OAuth are encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM) and are
used exclusively to act on our own account. No other party is granted access.

## Third-party services

The application interacts with the official APIs of Pinterest, Etsy, and Gumroad
solely to operate our own accounts on those platforms. Their respective privacy
policies govern the data processed on their systems.

## Data retention

We retain access tokens only for as long as the application is connected to our
accounts. Tokens can be revoked at any time from the respective platform.
